# Platform Comparison Summary

## Quick Overview

This document provides a quick reference summary of the comprehensive platform comparison. For full details, see [PLATFORM_COMPARISON.md](./PLATFORM_COMPARISON.md).

## The Core Problem

**85% of India's 146 million farmers don't use digital trading platforms due to language barriers.**

- Only 15% use eNAM after 7+ years
- 75% prefer local languages over English/Hindi
- 26% rural literacy rate in agricultural states
- Existing platforms (eNAM, ODOP, GeM) are English-centric

## Our Solution: Enhancement, Not Replacement

**Multilingual Mandi makes government platforms accessible through:**

1. **Voice-First Interface** - 6+ local languages (Hindi, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Punjabi)
2. **AI Negotiation** - Peer-to-peer with intelligent counter-offers
3. **Transparent Pricing** - Formula-based quality pricing
4. **Peer Discovery** - Farmer collaboration without middlemen
5. **Trust System** - Objective metrics and AI dispute resolution
6. **Government Integration** - eNAM prices, ODOP badges, GeM guidance
7. **Market Intelligence** - Advisory in local languages
